Scope

All key procurement topics included—kept intentionally lean.

Source-to-Contract (S2C)

  • Category strategy and sourcing pipeline
  • RFI / RFP, negotiation support, award governance
  • Supplier due diligence and qualification inputs
  • Contracting model and handover to P2P

Procure-to-Pay (P2P)

  • Requisition-to-PO workflow and approvals
  • Order confirmation, delivery evidence, invoice compliance
  • 3-way match, exceptions handling, audit trail
  • KPIs for cycle time and compliance

Supplier Governance

  • Vendor onboarding (data, documents, approvals)
  • Vendor assessment (risk, quality, financial, compliance)
  • Vendor classification (criticality, category, risk tier)
  • Supplier ratings and performance monitoring

ESG & Contract Management

  • ESG questionnaires and evidence collection
  • Supplier ESG scoring integrated into ratings
  • Contract lifecycle (create → approve → sign → renew)
  • Obligations, expiries, and compliance reporting
